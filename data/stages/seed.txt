Seed-stage funding focuses on early traction, product-market fit, and initial revenue.
Investors at this stage look for validated ideas and early customers.
Seed Stage Funding

Typical Characteristics:
- Product MVP exists
- Early user traction
- Small founding team

Typical Funding Range:
- $500K to $2M

Investor Expectations:
- Clear problem-solution fit
- Early revenue or engagement metrics
- Founding team credibility
